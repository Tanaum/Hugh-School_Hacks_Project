What Is The Issue?

You may be wondering that all of this climate change and global warming is something that people should not worry about since it is natural for the Earth to warm up. We wish that this was right, unfortunately, the data shows otherwise. Places that had never recieved any snowfall had to suffer due to it; summers are hotter than usual (there's a heat wave going on in several countries such as the UK and India). The ozone layer has been depleting due the harmful gases released from factories. Those same gases also contribute to acid rain!

What Can You Do?

Fortunately, there are innumerable things you can do to help better our planet's situation! Here's a short a list of some of the things you can do:

  • Raise awarness via social media platforms: it absolutely does not matter how big or small your following is, raising awarness can make a difference!
  • Use the 3R's: Reuse, Reduce, Recycle!
  • Plant more trees, plants, etc. so they can replace the carbon dioxide in the air with oxygen.
  • Walk, cycle, and use public transportation!
  • Try to repair items around your house instead of throwing them away.
  • Donate and promote to various organistions that are working on combating this issue!
